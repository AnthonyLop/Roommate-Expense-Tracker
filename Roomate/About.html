Welcome to RoomieFinance!

Welcome to the Roommate Expense Tracker designed to simplify and streamline shared expenses among roommates or suitemates on campus.

Whether it's groceries, furniture, or any shared expense for the dorm room, this website helps roommates split costs and maintain a list of needed items.

Project Overview

The Roommate Expense Tracker is a web application that facilitates tracking, splitting, and managing shared expenses. It allows roommates to log and split expenses such as rent, utilities, groceries, and more, providing a transparent and easy-to-use platform.

Key Features

Track bill deadlines and receive reminders
View all expenses in one place
Access historical data and reports
Deduct money for bills and meet savings goals
Add, edit, and delete expenses
Mark expenses as paid and ensure financial transparency
