About RoomieFinance

Welcome to RoomieFinance, your go-to roommate finance tracker! Living with roommates can be an amazing experience, but managing shared expenses can sometimes be challenging. That's where RoomieFinance comes in to make your financial life with roommates easier and more organized.

Our Mission

At RoomieFinance, we aim to simplify and streamline the way you and your roommates handle shared finances. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly platform that allows you to track expenses, split bills, and maintain a transparent and harmonious financial relationship with your roommates.

Key Features

    Expense Tracking: Keep a record of shared expenses and who owes what.

    Bill Splitting: Easily split bills among roommates and track each person's share.
